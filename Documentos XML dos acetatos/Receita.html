Rhubarb Cobbler made with bananas as the main sweetener.
It was delicious. Basicly it was

2 1/2 cups diced rhubarb
2 tablespoons sugar
2 fairly ripe bananas
1/4 teaspoon cinnamon
dash of nutmeg

Combine all and use as cobbler, pie, or crisp.
